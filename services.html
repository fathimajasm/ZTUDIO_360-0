What is your usual turnaround time?

Tunraround time varies depending on the package you choose to ensure quality. For the Basic package, it may take around 1-2 days. Packages including retouches, it may take around 6-13 days.

02
Can we request specific shots or provide a shot list?

Of course! your input and details for your request are welcome, I will do my best to incorporate those ideas into the shoot. This may be accepted depending what the chosen packatge provides.

03
How far in advance do we need to book your services?

Booking in advanced is strongly recommended. This gives both you - the client - and me - the photographer - time to prepare and plan for the shoot. This also allows me to prepare the equipment needed.

04
Payment duration to secure my booking

Payment is required within 48 hours of confirming our agreement. This ensures your date is reserved, allowing me to properly prepare for your event. You can conveniently make the payment through Gcash by contacting me after confirming a package

05
What is your cancellation and refund policy?

I have a flexible cancellation policy. If there's a need to reschedule or cancel, please notify me as soon as possible. I'll do my best to accommodate changes. As for refunds, they are considered on a case-by-case basis."

Are there any limitations on the usage of the photos?
